## **Quality Control Procedures**

### **Equipment Maintenance:**
- **Daily:** Visual inspection and functionality check
- **Weekly:** Calibration verification for glucometers
- **Monthly:** Complete inventory and maintenance schedule
- **Per Camp:** Pre-camp checklist completion

### **Data Quality Assurance:**
- **Real-time:** Dual data entry verification
- **Per Patient:** Data completeness check (95% threshold)
- **Daily:** Error rate assessment (<2% threshold)
- **Post-Camp:** Data cleaning and validation

### **Safety Protocols:**
- **Hand Hygiene:** Alcohol-based rub (60% ethanol) or soap/water
- **Sharps Safety:** Single-use lancets, immediate disposal
- **Waste Management:** Color-coded biohazard disposal
- **Emergency Response:** Clear escalation protocols
