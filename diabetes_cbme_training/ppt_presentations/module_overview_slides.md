## Slide 7: Regional Context

# **Indian Rural Health Context**

### **Epidemiological Data**
- 🌍 **India:** 77 million people with diabetes (2023)
- 🏘️ **Rural Areas:** 65% of total diabetes burden
- 📈 **Projection:** 93 million by 2030

### **Local Challenges**
- 🎯 **Under-diagnosis:** 57% of diabetics undetected in rural areas
- 🚑 **Limited Access:** PHC level management in remote villages
- 💰 **Economic Burden:** ₹6248 crore annual cost
- 🌽 **Cultural Factors:** Traditional healing practices, stigma
