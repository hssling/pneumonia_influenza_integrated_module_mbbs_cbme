# Pneumonia Treatment and Immunization CBME Module

## 🚀 **MBBS Phase 3 Part 1 Integration Class**

A comprehensive Competency-Based Medical Education (CBME) module on **Pneumonia Treatment and Immunization** designed specifically for MBBS Phase 3 Part 1 students.

## 📋 **Module Overview**

### **🎯 Educational Objectives:**
- Master evidence-based pneumonia treatment protocols
- Understand pneumococcal and influenza vaccination strategies
- Develop clinical skills for pneumonia diagnosis and management
- Strengthen immunization implementation and coverage
- Achieve competency in vaccine safety and adverse event management

### **🏥 Clinical Focus:**
- Community-acquired pneumonia (CAP)
- Hospital-acquired pneumonia (HAP)
- Pneumococcal disease prevention
- Influenza seasonality and pandemic preparedness

## 📚 **Curriculum Structure**

### **Unit 1: Core Pneumonia Concepts (Chapters 1-6)**
- Epidemiology, pathophysiology, clinical diagnosis
- Radiologic and microbiologic diagnosis
- Evidence-based antibiotic treatment guidelines

### **Unit 2: Management & Treatment (Chapters 7-8)**
- Severe and complicated pneumonia management
- Supportive care and adjunct therapies

### **Unit 3: Prevention & Vaccination (Chapters 9-15)**
- Vaccination strategies and implementation
- Pneumococcal and influenza immunization
- Vaccine safety, adverse events, and public confidence
- Program evaluation and monitoring

## 🎓 **Assessment Framework**

### **📊 Competencies Assessed:**
- **Knowledge (MCQ Tests)**: 100+ structured questions
- **Clinical Skills (OSCE/OSPE)**: Interactive case-based scenarios
- **Practical Application**: Workshop-based learning
- **Continuous Assessment**: Rubrics and evaluation forms
