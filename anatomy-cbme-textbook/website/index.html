CBME-Aligned Learning Approach

🧠

Knowledge Domains

Progressive development from anatomical structure identification to functional-clinical correlations

  • Basic anatomical knowledge (KS)
  • Structure-function relationships (KP)
  • Clinical applications (PC)
  • Communication skills (COM)
  • Professional attitudes (AET)
💼

Entrustment Scales

Five-level progression from observation to independent practice with full supervision

  • Level 1: Observes (little confidence)
  • Level 2: Assists with supervision
  • Level 3: Performs independently in supervised setting
  • Level 4: Performs independently in most situations
  • Level 5: Supervises others
🩺

Clinical Integration

Every anatomical concept linked to clinical practice through case studies and problem-based learning

  • Case-based scenarios
  • Clinical skill correlations
  • Pathological correlations
  • Diagnostic reasoning
