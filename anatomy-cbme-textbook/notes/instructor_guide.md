# Instructor's Guide: CBME Human Anatomy Textbook

## Overview
This comprehensive CBME-aligned anatomy textbook represents a complete pedagogical transformation from traditional memorization-based learning to competency-driven clinical education.

## Key Features for Educators

### CBME Competency Mapping
- **Knowledge Phase (KP)**: KS1-3 marking basic to advanced knowledge acquisition
- **Skill Phase (SP)**: Practice competence development and assessment
- **Patient Care Phase (PC)**: Integration with clinical care environments

### Facilitator Roles
- **Medical Expert**: Chapter content with clinical correlations
- **Communicator**: Patient education emphasis throughout
- **Collaborator**: Interdisciplinary case studies and communication
- **Scholar**: Evidence-based learning with digital resources
- **Advocate**: Professional development and patient safety focus

---

## Teaching Strategies

### Large Group Sessions
1. **Interactive Lectures**: Use QR codes and videos for engagement
2. **Clinical Correlation Sessions**: Case studies to link anatomy with pathology
3. **Assessment Reviews**: MCQ discussions with anatomical reasoning

### Small Group Learning
1. **Skill Stations**: OSCE-like sessions for examination practice
2. **Problem-Based Learning**: Clinical scenarios requiring anatomical reasoning
3. **Peer Teaching**: Student-led anatomy teaching sessions

### Technology Integration
1. **Digital Microscopy**: Virtual histology sessions
2. **3D Anatomy Models**: Interactive spatial understanding
3. **Simulation Labs**: Surgical procedures with anatomical guidance

---

## Assessment Framework

### Competency Milestones

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Knowledge Foundation**
- Basic anatomical structures identification
- Microscopic structure recognition
- Developmental processes understanding

**Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Clinical Correlation**
- Disease process anatomical explanations
- Imaging finding interpretations
- Clinical examination skills demonstration

**Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Application Excellence**
- Complex case management contribution
- Teaching and communication competence
- Professional development initiative

### Assessment Methods

**Formative Assessment**:
- Weekly quiz modules
- Skill station performances
- Case presentation capabilities

**Summative Assessment**:
- Comprehensive final examination
- OSCE station demonstrations
- Clinical placement evaluations
