# Chapters 5-14: Major Body Systems

## Chapter 5: Cardiovascular System

**Competency Statements**:
1. **PC.3**: Perform cardiovascular physical examination
2. **PC.4**: Interpret ECG with anatomical correlation
3. **COM.2**: Explain cardiac pathology in patient-friendly terms

**Key Clinical Correlations**:
- Heart murmurs: Valve dysfunction anatomy
- MI pain radiation: Nerve supply patterns
- ECG changes: Electrical conduction system

**CBME Case**: 65-year-old with chest pain radiating to left arm
- Anatomical correlation: Heart innervation via cardiac plexus
- Competency: Locates PMI, auscultates heart sounds

## Unified CBME Themes Across All Chapters

### Progressive Competency Development

**Foundation Level** (Chapters 1-4): Basic structure and function correlations
**Intermediate Level** (Chapters 5-14): System-specific clinical applications
**Advanced Level** (Chapters 15-18): Integration and independent practice

### Assessment Framework

**Knowledge Assessment**: MCQs testing factual recall and application
**Skills Assessment**: OSCE stations for practical competencies
**Attitude Assessment**: Reflective portfolios and 360-degree feedback
**Communication Assessment**: Patient interaction and team collaboration

### Digital Integration

**QR Codes**: Link to 3D anatomy models and surgical videos
**Interactive Modules**: Case-based learning scenarios
**Assessment Platforms**: Continuous competency monitoring
**Community Forums**: Peer learning and knowledge sharing

### Innovation Features

**Evidence-Based Updates**: Regular content revision based on new anatomical research
**Patient Perspectives**: Real patient videos explaining anatomical conditions
**Technology Integration**: VR dissection, AI-powered assessment
**Global Perspectives**: Cultural competence in anatomical education

### Quality Assurance

**Peer Review**: Expert validation of anatomical content and clinical correlations
**Student Feedback**: Continuous improvement based on learner needs
**Outcome Measurement**: Clinical competency achievement tracking
**Faculty Development**: Training educators in CBME methodology

---

This comprehensive CBME-aligned anatomy curriculum transforms traditional anatomical education into clinical competence development, ensuring medical students become doctors who understand how anatomical structure determines physiological function, pathological presentations, and therapeutic approaches.
