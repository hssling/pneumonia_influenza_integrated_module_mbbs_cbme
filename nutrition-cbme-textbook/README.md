## 🌟 Overview

This comprehensive textbook provides a complete CBME-compliant curriculum for clinical nutrition education, specifically designed for Indian medical colleges under the National Medical Commission framework. The resource integrates foundational nutritional sciences with advanced clinical applications, public health perspectives, and professional competencies.

### 🎯 Key Features

- **42 Chapters** organized in 7 comprehensive units
- **CBME Integration** with competency mapping and assessment methods
- **Interactive Learning** through case studies and practical scenarios
- **Indian Context** with reference to national programs and cultural considerations
- **Digital Platform** for seamless access and updates

## 🎓 Educational Framework

### **Competency-Based Medical Education (CBME)**

This textbook adheres to the National Medical Commission (NMC) CBME framework with:

- **Knowledge Domain:** Integration of nutritional science with clinical medicine
- **Skills Development:** Practical nutritional assessment and counseling techniques
- **Attitude Formation:** Patient-centered, culturally sensitive nutritional care
- **Integration:** Linking nutrition with biomedical sciences, clinical medicine, and community health

### **Assessment Methods**
- **Knowledge:** Written examinations, MCQs, theory discussions
- **Skills:** Objective Structured Clinical Examinations (OSCE), counseling simulations
- **Attitude:** Portfolio assessments, reflective practice entries
- **Integration:** Case-based discussions, multidisciplinary team exercises
