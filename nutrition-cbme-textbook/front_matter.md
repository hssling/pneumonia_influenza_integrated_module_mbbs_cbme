## About This Textbook

This comprehensive nutrition textbook has been developed following Competency-Based Medical Education (CBME) guidelines as implemented in Indian medical colleges under the National Medical Commission.

The content systematically covers:
- **Basic nutritional science** and biochemistry
- **Clinical nutrition across the human lifecycle**
- **Major nutritional disorders** and their management
- **Specialized clinical nutrition interventions**
- Evidence-based practice integrated with Indian healthcare context

**Educational Approach:** Each chapter includes learning objectives, clinical case scenarios, assessment methods, and references aligned with CBME graduate attributes.

**Clinical Relevance:** Emphasis on practical nutritional interventions that can be implemented in Indian healthcare settings, with consideration of cultural preferences, socioeconomic factors, and national health programs.

## CBME Framework Integration

This textbook adheres to the Competency-Based Medical Education framework implemented by the National Medical Commission (NMC) for Indian medical education.

### **Graduate Attributes Covered:**
- **Knowledge Domain:** Integration of nutritional science with clinical medicine
- **Skills Development:** Practical nutritional assessment and counseling techniques
- **Attitude Formation:** Patient-centered, culturally sensitive nutritional care
- **Integration:** Linking nutrition with biomedical sciences, clinical medicine, and community health

### **Assessment Methods:**
- Written examinations testing knowledge foundations
- Objective Structured Clinical Examinations (OSCE) for skill development
- Portfolio assessments for attitude and professional development
