## About This Learning Content

This comprehensive ECG learning resource has been developed using Competency-Based Medical Education (CBME) guidelines, integrating traditional textbook content with modern multimedia approaches and artificial intelligence-driven learning tools.

The content systematically covers:
- **ECG fundamentals** and technical recording principles
- **Wave morphology, intervals, and normal variants**
- **Pathological ECG patterns** and their clinical significance
- **Advanced correlations** with cardiac conditions and systemic diseases
- **AI-assisted interpretation** and predictive analytics

**Educational Approach:** Interactive learning with case-based scenarios, virtual simulations, adaptive quizzes, and AI-powered feedback systems.

**Clinical Focus:** Practical ECG interpretation skills that can be immediately applied in clinical settings, with emphasis on diagnostic accuracy and patient management.
