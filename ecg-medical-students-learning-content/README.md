## 📚 Curriculum Coverage

### 📋 Complete Chapter Index

#### **Unit 1: ECG Fundamentals** 🏗️
1. **Introduction to Electrocardiography** - Historical foundations and cardiac physiology
2. **Recording Technique & Lead Systems** - Professional ECG acquisition
3. **ECG Waves, Segments & Intervals** - Systematic interpretation framework
4. **Normal ECG Patterns & Variants** - Population norms and physiological variations

#### **Unit 2: Ischemic Heart Disease** 💔
5. **Coronary Artery Disease & Myocardial Ischemia** - Pathophysiology and ECG correlations
6. **Myocardial Infarction & ECG Evolution** - STEMI/NSTEMI differentiation and timeline
7. **Supraventricular Arrhythmias** - SVAs including AF and flutter analysis
8. **Heart Failure & Cardiomyopathies** - Electrical manifestations of structural disease

#### **Unit 3: Arrhythmias & Devices** ⚡
9. **Ventricular Arrhythmias** - VT/VF classification and management
10. **Conduction Blocks & Bradyarrhythmias** - AV blocks and pacing indications
11. **Device Therapy & Pacemakers** - CRT, ICD, cardiac resynchronization
12. **ECG in Special Conditions** - Geriatric, pediatric, athletic variations

#### **Unit 4: Advanced Applications** 🚨
13. **Critical Care & Emergency ECG** - Life-threatening conditions identification
14. **Sports Medicine & Athletic Heart** - Exercise physiology ECG patterns
15. **Toxicology & Drug Effects** - Medication-induced ECG changes
16. **ECG Interpretation Algorithms** - Computer-assisted diagnosis systems

#### **Unit 5: AI & Future Medicine** 🤖
17. **Clinical Cases Atlas** - Complex real-world ECG scenarios
18. **ECG Teaching & Assessment Methods** - Educational methodologies
19. **AI, ML & Digital Health Revolution** - Future of cardiac diagnostics
