## About This Teaching-Learning Module

This comprehensive pandemic module has been developed following Competency-Based Medical Education (CBME) guidelines as implemented in Indian medical colleges under the National Medical Commission. The module is designed to equip future healthcare professionals with the knowledge, skills, and attitudes required for effective pandemic preparedness and management.

The content systematically covers:
- **Foundations of pandemics** and epidemiology
- **COVID-19 pandemic experience** and lessons learned
- **Clinical management** of pandemic diseases
- **Public health response strategies** and implementation
- **Disaster management and biohazard preparedness**
- **Mental health and psychological aspects** of pandemics
- **Ethical considerations and medical decision-making**
- **Policy, advocacy, and global health perspectives**

**Educational Approach:** Each chapter includes learning objectives, clinical case scenarios, assessment methods, and references aligned with CBME graduate attributes.

**Global and Local Relevance:** Emphasis on international health regulations while addressing Indian healthcare context, including national disaster management frameworks and healthcare delivery systems.

## Importance of Pandemic Education in Medical Curriculum

### **Why Pandemics Matter in Medical Education:**

1. **Historic and Contemporary Threats:**
   - COVID-19 (SARS-CoV-2): 6.9 million deaths globally (WHO, 2024)
   - Potential for future pathogens: Avian influenza (H5N1), Nipah virus, emerging coronaviruses
   - Antimicrobial resistance and emerging infectious diseases

2. **Indian Context:**
   - Kerala Nipah outbreaks (2018-2021)
   - Mumbai plague outbreak (1994)
   - National preparedness for pandemics as per Disaster Management Act 2005

3. **Global Health Security:**
   - International Health Regulations (2005)
   - One Health approach to zoonotic diseases
   - WHO pandemic preparedness frameworks

### **CBME Relevance:**
- **Competency Development:** Integration with NMC graduate attributes
- **Skill-Based Learning:** Hands-on training in pandemic response
- **Attitude Formation:** Ethical preparedness and leadership in crisis
- **Integrated Learning:** Connecting clinical medicine with public health
